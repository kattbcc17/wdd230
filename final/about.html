About us

Our food production is one of the most popular in Carlsbad, California.

Once upon a time, two girls named Emilia and Laura were dedicated to living a healthy lifestyle and consuming fresh, natural products. They observed a void in the market for a local juice bar that offered truly fresh and organic options, so they decided to fill it. Emilia and Laura combined their resources and founded a modest juice bar in Carlsbad, California, in 2010. They collected the freshest fruits and vegetables from local farms and crafted delicious and nutritious juice blends. The community's response was amazing, and their juice bar immediately became a neighborhood favorite. As their business expanded, Emilia and Laura decided to add smoothies, acai bowls, and other healthy snacks to their menu. They also pledged to be environmentally friendly, utilizing eco-friendly packaging and collaborating with local organizations to prevent food waste. Over a decade later, Emilia and Laura's juice bar has become a Carlsbad institution. They have many sites throughout the city and a devoted following of clients who value their devotion to health, sustainability, and community involvement.
